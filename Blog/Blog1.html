Top 5 Must-Visit Places in India for 2025

Meghalaya – The Hidden Paradise of the Northeast

Explore lush green landscapes, crystal-clear rivers, and stunning waterfalls. Must-see attractions include the Living Root Bridges in Cherrapunji and the Dawki River.

Best Time to Visit: October–April

Ladakh – The Land of High Passes

Experience rugged landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and thrilling road trips. Highlights include Pangong Lake and the Manali to Leh Bike Trip.

Best Time to Visit: May–September

Varanasi – The Spiritual Capital of India

Immerse yourself in ancient ghats, mesmerizing Ganga Aarti, and rich cultural heritage. Don't miss the Boat Ride on the Ganges and the Kashi Vishwanath Temple.

Best Time to Visit: October–March

Andaman & Nicobar Islands – The Maldives of India

Discover pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. Key attractions include Radhanagar Beach and Scuba Diving at North Bay & Neil Island.

Best Time to Visit: October–May

Rann of Kutch – India’s White Desert

Experience the vast white salt desert and the colorful Rann Utsav festival. Visit the White Salt Desert and Bhuj & Mandvi Beach.

Best Time to Visit: November–February
